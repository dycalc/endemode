ABOUT US

Shanghai Demode Industrial Corporation Ltd. was established in 2008 in Shanghai, with a registered capital of RMB One Million Yuan; its unified social credit code is 91310116682248336W. The company, located in Chenjia Town, Chongming District, Shanghai and having more than 50 full-time employees, is a comprehensive clothing manufacturer, and processing and wholesale factory, with its businesses covering clothing apparel, and fabrics and accessories producing and processing, knitwear and textiles, non-woven products, daily necessities, groceries, household supplies, beddings, mother and baby products, home textiles, feather products, cloth products, etc.
The company has set up a factory in Ningbo, Zhejiang Province, and also established long-term and solid cotton yarn supplying and cotton knitwear manufacturing bases in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shanghai, Guangdong, Anhui and other places. Meanwhile, it has enjoyed a long-term and stable two-way cooperative relationship with other companies, providing an effective guarantee for the company’s sustainable development.
Among our major brand cooperators are IT, DSP, Xlarge and HardyHardy; some of these brands’ import and export businesses have further been undertaken by us. In 2020, our annual production of garments hit 1 million pieces despite the impact of the epidemic, and our average annual turnover has increased by some 10% to 30% over the years.
With the systematic enterprise management implemented for a long time, the company boasts a powerful management, which ensures active communication and collaboration between departments and their strict observance of the company’s rules and regulations, thereby providing guarantee and impetus for the company’s profit maximization and sustainable development.
